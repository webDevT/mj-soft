introduction

We want to save your money

MJ-Soft is an association of several freelancers under one roof, an association that allows a very large room for maneuver in carrying out projects, no project is too big and no project is too small.

The amount of people involved in the project depends solely on the size of the project and the customer's requirements, there is no need to "feed" and "run" unnecessary people, which saves our customers a lot of money and allows us to give very attractive offers.

Your project is our project

MJ-Soft specializes in the development of websites and mobile applications for a wide range of areas and requirements, from image sites for the self-employed and small businesses to complex eCommerce applications. The wide range of technologies we work with allows us to tailor the perfect solution for each customer.

MJ-Soft believes in close supervision and full cooperation with customers, your project is our project, we are attentive to all the customer's concerns and considerations and work hard to meet its requirements.

The company is located in the high-tech park "Gev Yam Negev" in Be'er Sheva.

As part of the Negev development vision, the company provides significant benefits to businesses in the south and to the residents of the Negev.
